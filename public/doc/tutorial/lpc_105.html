2.5.4 Destroy all non-interactive objects in an object

This is a handy command for clearing up a cluttered room. Usually something that you'd want to do before inviting a mortal to your workroom. I just wish it was this easy in Real Life (tm)...

Syntax: Clean <room spec>

Please notice that all non-interactive objects, i.e. monsters and gadgets alike, are destroyed.
